How to use:

Just fill in your details in the form and leave the rest on us. Sit back and relax until you get further notification from the TPO.

Yes it’s that easy!
Happy Interning!!

How the software works:

The moment you fill in your details and click on submit a mail is sent to the HoD of your department to verify your details and at the same time you receive a confirmation at your personal mail id.
• The HoD might approve or deny your request. You will be notified accordingly.
• If approved, your details will be forwarded to the TPO.
• After receiving your request the TPO will process your certificates.
• Once they generate your certificates you will receive a mail. You then just need to go and collect them from the TPO.
